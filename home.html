Hey there!

I believe that individuals can create a positive impact in whichever capacity they find themselves; so far, they stay dedicated and committed. I have expressed these qualities throughout my professional journey. I have stayed dogged regardless of challanges, giving my 100 and one percent, taking initiatives whenever the situation requires it, and constantly expressing the desire for a better world through leadership.

While I have a background in Statistics, I have obtained experiences in different fields, mainly in the non- profit sector.
I am currently the Country Director for AIESEC in Malawi. Before obtaining that position, I moved through various levels of the organization, from managing a small team on a local level in Nigeria and heading the Operations department, to managing national Operations in Ethiopia and building sustainablee organizational structures in Malawi.
I am a freelance writer with 3 years of experience and written over 100 clients and websites. Currently, I am pushing for more awareness in Nigeria on climate change.
